Gestures (Right Click +)

    Left: Go to previous page in history
    Right: Go to next page in history
    Up→Left: Go to parent directory
    Up→Down: Refresh page
    Up: Stop loading
    Down (w/ link): Open link in a new tab
    Down (w/o link): Open a new tab
    Down→Up (w/ link): Open link in a new background tab
    Down→Up (w/o link): Open a copy in a background tab
    Wheel: Switch active tab
    Down→Right: Close current tab
    Down→Left: Minimize window
    Up→Right: Maximize window
    Right→Up: Restore recently closed tab/window (Extra Function; disabled in default)
